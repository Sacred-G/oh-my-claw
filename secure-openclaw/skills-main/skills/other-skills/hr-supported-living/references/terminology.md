# Terminology Guide for Supported Living Services

## Correct vs. Incorrect Terms

### People We Serve
- ✅ **Consumer** - Correct term
- ❌ Client, Patient, Resident - Avoid these terms

### Staff Roles
- ✅ **Direct Support Staff (DSS)** - Correct term
- ❌ Direct Support Professional (DSP) - Do not use
- ✅ **Program Manager** or **Program Coordinator**
- ✅ **IT Director**
- ✅ **HR Manager** or **HR Director**

### Service Type
- ✅ **Supported Living Services (SLS)**
- ✅ **Independent Living Services (ILS)**
- ✅ **Community-based services**
- ❌ Residential care, group home services - Not applicable

### Regulatory Bodies
- ✅ **Title 17** - Community Care Licensing regulations
- ❌ Title 22 - NOT applicable to supported living
- ✅ **Regional Center** - California's developmental services system
- ✅ **Community Care Licensing (CCL)**
- ✅ **Department of Developmental Services (DDS)**

### Disabilities and Conditions
- ✅ **Intellectual disability** - Current preferred term
- ❌ Mental retardation, developmentally disabled - Outdated terms
- ✅ **Behavioral support needs**
- ✅ **Co-occurring conditions**
- ✅ **Person-first language** - "Consumer with autism" not "autistic consumer"

### Service Locations
- ✅ **Consumer's own home**
- ✅ **Natural settings**
- ✅ **Community settings**
- ❌ Facility, institution, placement - Avoid institutional language

### Documentation Terms
- ✅ **Individual Service Plan (ISP)** or **Individual Program Plan (IPP)**
- ✅ **Progress notes** or **Service notes**
- ✅ **Incident report**
- ✅ **Medication observation log** (not administration log)

### California-Specific Terms
- ✅ **Vendored agency** - Organization contracted with Regional Center
- ✅ **Purchase of Service (POS)** - Regional Center contracting standards
- ✅ **Lanterman Act** - California law governing developmental services
- ✅ **Person-Centered Planning (PCP)**

## Common Abbreviations
- **SLS** - Supported Living Services
- **ILS** - Independent Living Services
- **DSS** - Direct Support Staff
- **ISP** - Individual Service Plan
- **IPP** - Individual Program Plan
- **CCL** - Community Care Licensing
- **DDS** - Department of Developmental Services
- **POS** - Purchase of Service
- **PCP** - Person-Centered Planning
- **HIPAA** - Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
- **ADA** - Americans with Disabilities Act
